# Mission Board Test Checklist

Use this checklist before tagging or sharing a Mission Board release.

## Mission Brief

- Mission Brief appears below the sync/status bar and does not cover controls.
- Expanded Mission Brief collapses after about 9 seconds.
- The `Mission Brief` pill reopens the brief.
- Close collapses the brief immediately.
- Expanded Mission Brief appears no more than once per local calendar day per device.
- The same message does not appear twice in a row on the same device.
- Professional and Friendly tones contain no movie-riff lines.
- Deckplate Banter uses curated local messages only.

## Badge Display

- Mission Board ranks by score.
- Weekly winners and the selected month winner appear above the ranked list.
- In-progress periods are labeled as current leaders, not final winners.
- Each row also shows meaningful visits, distinct units, recovered units, and active days.
- Badge display stays compact on a phone.
- Tapping a row or badge shows plain-language badge criteria.
- Users with no badges are not shamed or called out.

## Badge Eligibility

- First Rounds appears after the member's first qualifying monthly check-in.
- Recovery Team appears after a recovered overdue or never-visited unit.
- Gray to Green appears after a first-ever unit visit.
- Wide Coverage appears after five distinct units in the month.
- Sustained Presence appears after qualifying check-ins on four distinct local days.
- Coverage Sweep appears only when area coverage has no overdue or never-visited active units remaining.
- Voided check-ins do not contribute to badges.

## Post-Check-In Celebration

- New badge celebration appears inside the confirmation screen, not as a modal or overlay.
- At most two new badges appear.
- More than two badges show `+N more on Mission Board`.
- Done remains immediately available.
- Repeated sync or page reload does not duplicate the same monthly celebration.

## Offline

- Mission Brief works from cached coverage.
- Offline queued check-ins do not show fake badge awards before sync.
- After sync, Mission Board reflects the uploaded visit once.
- Repeated Sync Now does not duplicate points or badge celebrations.
