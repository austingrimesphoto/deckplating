# Deckplating Data Dictionary

Deckplating is designed to reduce risk, not to claim approval. This dictionary supports local IT/N6, privacy, records, OPSEC, and command review.

| Field/table | Purpose | Example | Allowed/prohibited content | PII/CUI/sensitivity assessment | Retention recommendation | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `organizations.name`, `organizations.slug` | Identify a controlled demonstration workspace | `example-rmt` | Allowed: non-sensitive command/workspace label. Prohibited: classified/CUI/sensitive mission names. | Low-sensitivity administrative metadata if non-sensitive. | Delete after demonstration unless approved retention exists. | Not proof of command approval. |
| `app_settings` | Store workspace display/map/tone settings | `installation_name`, map center | Allowed: public/general installation or area. Prohibited: restricted or deployed operational locations. | Location sensitivity depends on content. | Delete with workspace. | Use general map center only. |
| `areas.name` | Broad grouping for coverage scanning | `Waterfront` | Allowed: broad non-sensitive area. Prohibited: restricted room, SCIF, or sensitive space. | Usually low sensitivity if broad. | Delete with workspace. | Keep coarse. |
| `locations.name`, coordinates, radius | Public/general mapped locations | `Pier Admin`, general building coordinate | Allowed: public/general locations. Prohibited: SCIFs, residences, deployed locations, restricted rooms. | Potential OPSEC/location sensitivity. | Delete with workspace; review before demo. | Leave sensitive places unmapped. |
| `units.name`, `unit_type`, interval | Coverage targets | `Engineering Department` | Allowed: non-sensitive department/division/tenant label. Prohibited: sensitive mission details. | Low to moderate sensitivity depending on label. | Delete with workspace. | Avoid mission-specific naming. |
| `team_members.name`, `role` | Local accountability and PIN selection | `CH Smith`, `RP Lead` | Allowed: minimum practical display identity. Prohibited: phone, DOB, family details, private contact info. | Low-sensitivity administrative identity data; may be PII. | Delete when member leaves demo; delete workspace after demo. | Do not claim “no sensitive PII.” |
| `team_members.pin_hash` | Authenticate local app users | Hash value | Allowed: hash only. Prohibited: plaintext PIN. | Security-sensitive credential derivative. | Delete with member/workspace. | Never export or display. |
| `devices.device_token_hash` | Bind browser/device sessions | Hash value | Allowed: hash only. Prohibited: plaintext token. | Security-sensitive token derivative. | Delete on reset/signout/workspace deletion. | Excluded from safe export. |
| `checkin_batches`, `checkins` | Generic visit/check-in event | Timestamp, unit, visitor display name | Allowed: visit occurred, unit/location visited, timestamp, visitor display name. Prohibited: notes, counseling, case, medical, incident details. | Low to moderate sensitivity; may include display-name PII. | Short demonstration retention; define locally. | Not an official record. |
| `checkin_batches.confidential_care_provided`, `referral_provided` | Legacy optional flags | `null` | Navy-facing default: disabled. Prohibited: details or case facts. | Risk of misinterpretation; leave disabled. | Do not use unless specifically approved. | Kept for backward compatibility only. |
| `operator_audit_events` | Operator accountability | `workspace_created` | Allowed: action, timestamp, workspace slug, minimal status. Prohibited: setup-code plaintext, passphrases, sensitive notes. | Administrative/security metadata. | Retain only as needed for demonstration accountability. | Notification logs must not store setup code plaintext. |
| `workspace_requests` | Manual approval queue | administrative contact email, use case | Allowed: official administrative contact email and non-sensitive request details. Prohibited: personal email if avoidable, phone, sensitive details. | Email may be PII; request text may become sensitive if misused. | Delete rejected/stale requests promptly. | Warn users before submission. |
| `organization_setup_codes.code_hash` | One-time activation setup | Hash value | Allowed: hash only. Prohibited: plaintext in logs/docs/screenshots. | Security-sensitive credential derivative. | Expire quickly; delete/revoke after use. | Plaintext appears only at issue time to operator/approved contact if intentionally sent. |
| Feedback forms | Demonstration support | bug report | Allowed: non-sensitive setup/UX feedback. Prohibited: setup codes, passphrases, screenshots with production data, CUI, counseling, medical, operational details. | Depends on submitter content; treat as potentially sensitive. | Review and delete unnecessary submissions. | Hosted setup-site forms are not official records systems. |
